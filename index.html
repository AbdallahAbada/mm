WELCOME TO MANCHESTER MEDICS

We provide fast, reliable, high-quality care within the local community, led by a team of longstanding consultants from the North of the UK.

High-Quality and Timely Healthcare in Your Community

Manchester Medics, proud of our NHS heritage, are a CQC approved group of consultants delivering high-quality care across various specialties. We are dedicated to improving healthcare access and outcomes in local communities by collaborating with GP practices, Clinical Commissioning Groups, and Acute Hospital Trusts. Our goal is to provide patient-centered, evidence-based care, strengthening ties with local clinical teams and delivering services directly where people live and work.
